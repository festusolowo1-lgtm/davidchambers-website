Staff Roles Explained
Super Admin
Full access to everything — settings, pricing, team, logo, bank details, all cases, all staff management.
Case Manager
Can manage cases, update timelines, send messages to clients, add invoices and upload documents. Cannot access settings.
Receptionist
Can view and confirm bookings, read and reply to contact inquiries. Cannot access cases or settings.
